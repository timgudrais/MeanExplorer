10 philosophies

How do I start amassing wealth?

  1. The power of spirit - Find a reason greater than reality
    • I don't want to work all my life
    • I don't want to be an employee
    • I want to be free to travel the world
    • I want to have guarantee a certain quality of lifestyle
    • I want control over my time and my life
  2. The power of choice - Make daily choices
    • Poor people have poor spending habits
    • Choose to be rich
    • Aquire assets, real assets
    • Invest first in financial education
    • IN and out of stocks, long on education
  3. The power of friends - Choose friends carefully
    • Learn from all of them
    • Friends in dire financial situations don't talk about money
    • Friends well off talk about it, out of interest
    • Don't listen to scared people
    • There is always another wave
    • Smart investors get themselves in position for the next wave
    • Buy when it's not popular
    • Don't time the market, reverse it
    • Rich friends keep track of the market
  4. The power of learning quickly - Master a formula, then learn a new one
    • You become what you study
    • Basic is work for money
    • Buy real-esstate foreclosure
    • Finding faster formulas is key
  5. The power of self-discipline - Pay yourself first
    • The number one delineator between rich and poor
    • Management of cash flow
    • Management of people
    • Management of personal time
  6. The power of good advice - Pay your brokers well
    • Pay professionals well and let them make you money
    • How much stocks do they own, hwo much tax do they pay
    • Reward people who are smarter than you
  7. The power of getting something from nothing - Be an indian giver
    • How fast do I get my money back?
    • What do I get for free?
    • ROI is very important
    • Putting money into a stock about to move
    • Pulling out the initial investment after about a month
    • Only play with money I can afford to lose
    • Limied risk/low risk-idea
    • What assets do I get for free once I get my money back
    • Ex. Stock, house, office building, condominium, mini-storage, piece of land
  8. The power of focus - Use assets to buy luxuries
    • The easy road becomes hard, the hard road becomes easy
  9. The power of myth - Find your heroes
    • Copy or emulate your heroes
    • Learn from mentors that know much more than you do
  10. The power of giving - Teach and you shall recieve
    • Be generous with what you have and it will come back to you

12 actions

  1. Stop doing what you're doing
    • Assess what is working and what is not
  2. Look for new ideas
    • Formulas, how to invest in X
    • 16% solution
  3. Find someone who has done what you want to do
    • Ask them how
  4. Take classes, read and attend seminars
    • Invest in knowledge
  5. Make lots of offers
    • Offer low, try to deal
    • Someone might say yes
  6. Make offers with escape clauses
    • Approval of business partner
  7. Go to the market
    • Search
    • Offer
    • Reject
    • Negotiate
    • Accept
  8. Walk/Jog/Drive a neighborhood 10 minutes every month
    • For a year
    • Look for change
    • Postal carriers
    • New retailers
    • Moving trucks
  9. Shop for bargains in all markets
    • Foreclosure auction
    • Find class on foreclosure
  10. Look for people who want to buy, then look for someone who sells
    • Buy the pie and cut it in pieces
  11. Think big
    • Negotiating a deal is easier in bulk
    • Use friends/Allies to engage bigger deals
  12. Learn from history
    • Action always beats inaction
